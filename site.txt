Website Design Prompt for “Erol Mobilya & Koltuk” — erollmobilya.com

Design an elegant, modern, and refined website for Eroll Mobilya & Koltuk, a furniture and sofa manufacturer. The visual style should feel high-quality, warm, and sophisticated.

Brand & Colors

Brand Name: Eroll Mobilya & Koltuk

Domain: erollmobilya.com

Main Color (Accent): #B19877 — use this for important UI elements like header highlights, key buttons, and section accents.

Black: #0a0908

White (Background): #fefae0

Add 2–3 complementary colors in the same palette range (muted beige, warm gray, or soft brown tones) for buttons and UI elements.

Design Style

Modern, elegant, and clean

White background with black text, complemented by the main gold-beige tone

High-quality furniture/sofa imagery

Subtle shadows, rounded edges, and premium typography

Overall aesthetic should feel luxurious yet minimal

Page Structure
1. Homepage

Medium-sized slider/hero section showcasing sofa sets and furniture

“Our Services” section with icons or minimal cards

A product preview section featuring 3–4 selected sofa sets or furniture products

Clear CTA buttons using the accent color

Elegant header and footer

2. About Us (Hakkımızda)

Company background, craftsmanship emphasis, mission of quality

3. Vision & Mission

Separate page explaining long-term goals and core values

4. Sofa Sets (Oturma Grupları) — Category Page

Grid layout of sofa set collections

Each item links to its own detailed product page

5. Individual Sofa Set Pages

Large product images

Description, dimensions, materials, and customization options

6. Contact Page

Contact form

Map, phone number, email, and WhatsApp button

General Notes

Use the main color #B19877 for emphasis and primary CTA buttons

Maintain consistency, spacing, and premium feel

Navigation should be clean, minimal, and clearly structured

Ensure responsiveness across devices

Include a visually appealing header and a neat footer with links and contact info
